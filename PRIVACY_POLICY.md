# TruthBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-24

TruthBot is a WhatsApp-based fact-checking assistant. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and what choices users have.

## 1) Who we are

TruthBot is operated by the app owner ("we", "us", "our").  
Contact: `sbansal1993@gmail.com`

## 2) What data we process

When you interact with TruthBot on WhatsApp, we may process:

- Message content you send (text, links, image/video captions)
- Media you send for analysis (such as images or videos)
- Technical metadata required for operation (timestamps, message type)
- WhatsApp identifier and phone-derived identifier (phone values may be stored as hashed identifiers where applicable)
- Feedback you submit (e.g., helpful/not helpful/wrong and optional reason)
- Usage and performance data (for reliability and debugging)

We do not request sensitive personal data unless you voluntarily include it in content you send.

## 3) Why we process data

We process data to:

- Analyze claims and generate fact-check responses
- Provide source-backed verdicts
- Improve quality, safety, and reliability
- Monitor abuse/rate limits and operational health
- Troubleshoot service errors

## 4) Legal basis / consent context

By messaging TruthBot, you instruct us to process submitted content for fact-checking and service delivery.

## 5) Third-party processors

TruthBot may use third-party services to process requests, including:

- Meta WhatsApp Cloud API (message transport)
- OpenAI (claim extraction/reasoning)
- Tavily (web/article search and extraction)
- Google APIs (Fact Check Tools, Vision OCR)
- Hive (AI-generated/deepfake detection)
- Hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g., Railway)

These providers may process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

## 6) Data retention

We retain data only as needed for service operation, diagnostics, and quality improvement.

- Cached verdict data may be retained for limited periods (e.g., TTL-based caching)
- Feedback/usage records may be retained for analytics and product improvement

We aim to minimize retained personal data and use hashed identifiers where possible.

## 7) Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.  
We share data only with service providers required to operate TruthBot, as described above.

## 8) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.  
However, no system is 100% secure.

## 9) User rights and deletion

You can request deletion of your TruthBot-related data by following the instructions on the Data Deletion page:

- `DATA_DELETION.md` in this repository (or its published URL)

For requests, contact: `sbansal1993@gmail.com`

## 10) Children's privacy

TruthBot is not intended for use by children below the age required by applicable law.

## 11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at the same URL with an updated date.
